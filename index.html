Mercury

  • Size: The smallest planet in our solar system, with a diameter of about 3,032 mi
  • Distance from the sun: 36.04 million mi
  • Fun Facts: Mercury is the mallest planet in the solar system and it takes 88 Earth days to complete. It travels around the sun at nearly 29 miles per second.

Venus

  • Size: Its surface area is 177.7 million mi²
  • Distance from the sun: 67.63 million mi
  • Fun Facts: Venus has 90 times the atmospheric pressure of Earth which makes Venus very hot. Venus is also named after the roman god of love.

Earth

  • Size: 7,926 miles (12,760 kilometers).
  • Distance from the sun: 93 million miles (150 million kilometers) away
  • Fun Facts: Earth is the only planet known to support life

  • Distance from the sun:146.34 million mi
  • Fun Facts: Mars has two moons, and their names are Phobos and Deimos.
